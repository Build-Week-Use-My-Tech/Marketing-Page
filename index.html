Welcome To Use My Tech Stuff

Tired of paying ridiculous fees for camera and other equipment rentals? Bypass the middleman and rent from a real person!

How It Works

The users can either rent any type of equipment you wish to rent or you can lend out your equipment. They can set up items they have for rent such as cameras, TVs, Party equipment like speakers/fog machines etc.

Save Money

As a renter, you will be able to save a lot of money by not needing to buy the equipment when you only need to use the equipment for a short amount of time.

Make Money

As a lender, you will be able to make income by lending out your equipment to thousands of renters who really need to use the equipment but would like to save money or can't afford to buy one.

User Features

Lenders will be able to Create, Read, Update and Delete rental data. Renters can log in and see items that users have for rent and ask to rent an item.
